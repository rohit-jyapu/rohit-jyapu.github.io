Bhutan Trips - Small Group Journeys

Small Group trips or Journeys are for people who share our passion for the total experience of Bhutan and beyond – the cuisine, the natural beauty, the history, culture and people. We do small group travel differently but don’t cut corners and yet offer you great value. Most of Group trips have maximum limit of 8 and some are even smaller. Because the groups are so small, we’re able to accommodate individual interests by allowing plenty of opportunities for independent exploration. These small group trips to Bhutan are scheduled to coincide with local festivals / events and are escorted by most sought-after native English speaking Tour Leaders who are local experts with connections.

You can take any of our Small Group Journeys and create an independent private trip with your own dates or choose from the collection of our Private Journeys.

Join-in Group is a small group trip, that begins when one or two call to set up a tour for themselves according to their wishes and invite others to join.
